About Skon

Behind the lens: Skandar Wanan

Skandar is a freelance photographer based in Tunisia, focusing on real, unscripted emotions and cinematic light. Whether it is a wedding day, a portrait session, or a private event, his goal is to make every image feel honest and alive.

Skon Photography blends storytelling with clean, modern editing—so your photos look good today and still feel timeless years from now, on screens or in printed albums.
